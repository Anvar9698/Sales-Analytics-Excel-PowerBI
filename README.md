# 📊 Sales & Cost Analysis Dashboard

This project demonstrates how **Excel** and **Power BI** can be combined to analyze business performance and visualize insights.  
The dataset was prepared and cleaned in **Excel**, then transformed into interactive dashboards using **Power BI**.

---

## 🌍 Sales by Country
Treemap visualization highlights the distribution of total sales:
- **United States**: Largest share  
- **Canada & France**: Moderate contributions  

## 🛒 Sales by Product Category
Horizontal bar chart shows clear dominance of the **Bikes** category:
- Bikes: $66.30M  
- Components: $11.80M  
- Clothing: $1.78M  
- Accessories: $0.57M  

## 💰 Average Standard Cost
Gauge chart indicates an **average cost of 454.41** on a scale up to 908.82.  
Useful for benchmarking production and pricing strategies.

## 📉 Discounts vs Order Quantity
Line chart reveals a steep decline in discounts as order quantity increases.  
Insight: Larger orders tend to receive fewer discounts, reflecting pricing discipline.

---

### 🔎 Key Insights
- The U.S. market drives the majority of sales.  
- Bikes are the most profitable category.  
- Discounts decrease significantly with higher order volumes.  
- Average cost benchmarks can guide pricing decisions.
